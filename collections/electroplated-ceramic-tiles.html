Collection: Electroplated Ceramic Tiles

Home   |   Collections   |   Electroplated Ceramic Tiles

Electroplated ceramic tiles are fired at high temperatures and have a full and rich surface color. With its futuristic sense of technology, this characteristic material can inspire users’ creativity and imagination about color, making the space avant-garde and bold. The paving is very simple, and the joints can show a natural and smooth transition. Whether on the ceiling or facades such as interior and exterior walls, electroplated ceramic tiles can show wonderful decorative effects.
