Microcement

What is Microcement

A decorative coating with the appearance of natural stone composed of high-quality minerals, cements, resins, additives and mineral pigments.

Microcement's properties

Strong adhesion

It can be applied to both vertical and horizontal surfaces.

Microcement's properties

No limitations

Microcement's properties

Thin and delicate

Microcement's properties

No seams

Easy constructions

Microcement is not a single layer of coating. Each layer has its own significance, making the covered area waterproof, moisture-proof and more durable, combining beauty and practicality.

Rich color & surface

32 popular colors
3 texture effects: Small, Medium and Coarse

Where can microcement be used?

It is commonly used for indoor and outdoor floors and walls, and can also be used in wet areas such as kitchens, showers, swimming pools, etc.
