About Us

Welcome to PANELPIX

At PANELPIX, we're more than just a supplier of architectural materials. We are your partners in realizing the creative and engineering vision of your projects. Through innovation, quality, and a commitment to sustainability, we provide you with the perfect solutions for every detail of your design.

We believe that the smallest details make the biggest difference, which is why we offer premium, eco-friendly building materials that meet the highest standards of excellence. With PANELPIX, the possibilities are limitless. Let us help you turn your architectural dreams into an astonishing reality, while preserving our planet for future generations.

PANELPIX is a leader in sourcing innovative, high-quality, and eco-friendly architectural materials. We serve designers, architects, construction firms, design studios, and all professionals within the architectural design industry. We simplify every stage of the interior and exterior design process, ensuring exceptional products and outstanding service.

We offer a vast range of products sourced from a global network of suppliers.

Reach out to us, and you'll gain access to exclusive material guides, annual building material trend collections, and detailed price lists from PANELPIX

 

You can choose directly from our wide range of new material options, or provide your ideas and models to our business staff.

  • Why Choose PANELPIX

Innovative and Eco-Friendly Materials

At PANELPIX, we are committed to providing building materials that combine innovation, quality, and sustainability. With our global network of suppliers, we deliver eco-friendly materials that meet the highest standards, helping to create stunning and sustainable architectural designs.

Superior Quality Products

Our meticulously selected products come from the finest factories worldwide. Our expert team visits each factory to verify the quality, ensuring that you receive precise and exceptional products. We offer a diverse range of materials, including panels, components, tiles, paper, weavings, and photoelectric items, to meet all your design needs.

Cost-Effective Solutions

We understand the importance of balancing quality and cost. Through our efficient procurement process, we achieve additional savings by partnering with factories that produce high-quality products at competitive prices. This way, we help you achieve client satisfaction while reducing operational expenses.

Flexible Delivery Options

We strive to ensure that your products are delivered on time according to your schedule. We offer flexible delivery options tailored to your needs, collaborating with designers or contractors to define production requirements and timelines accurately.

Partnership and Design Support

We work closely with your procurement team to ensure seamless and efficient project management. Our development and design experts are dedicated to meeting your expectations and aesthetic standards, guaranteeing exceptional results.

Exceptional Customer Service

We place great importance on meeting each client's needs with precision and professionalism. We maintain constant communication throughout the process, with our specialized procurement and quality control teams overseeing production, packaging, and shipping, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free purchasing experience.

Sustainability Commitment

We aim to make the world a better place by providing eco-friendly and sustainable building materials. We recognize the importance of preserving the environment for future generations, which is why we strive to offer products that minimize environmental impact and encourage sustainable use of natural resources. By promoting the use of renewable and environmentally friendly products, we work towards a more sustainable future.

Outstanding Partnerships

We take pride in fostering strong, long-term partnerships with industry-leading manufacturers. These relationships enable us to provide customized solutions that meet the needs of clients in interior and exterior design, ensuring innovative and cutting-edge designs.

The PANELPIX Team

At PANELPIX, we take immense pride in our team of outstanding professionals and experts who blend deep expertise with boundless passion in procurement, business, and construction materials. Our remarkable team is composed of creative and specialized minds dedicated to delivering the highest levels of quality and innovation, ensuring the commercial success of our clients and partners. Through clear and effective communication, we gain profound insights into our customers' needs, providing bespoke procurement solutions that guarantee excellence, competitiveness, and cost efficiency. Our team also meticulously oversees production and logistics to ensure timely delivery, keeping projects on track and running smoothly.
